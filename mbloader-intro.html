What is MB Loader?

MB Loader is an Android app similar to Toolbox that allows user to open Minecraft with their favorite shader loader (MBL2 and Draco)

Means no need to download patched APK anymore. Both paid and crack MC players can use it.

More details can be found on the YSS Discord server (just ask in #android or #general)
